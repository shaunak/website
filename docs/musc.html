Some Musical Stuff I Do

I was a member of Eh? Cappella, a Canadian Acapella group in the 2018-2019 school year. We learned and sang a bunch of fun, exciting songs that I'll honestly never see the same again, and competed with a four of those songs in the ICCA's (the singing contest from Pitch Perfect!)
We made it to Semifinals in Salem, OR.
